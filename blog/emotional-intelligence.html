Emotional intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ refers to a person's "emotional intelligence" it represents a person's emotional intelligence, that is, a person's ability to manage one's own emotions and manage the emotions of others. It depends on whether a person is passionate about work and life, whether he is a person with "sympathy" and "sensitivity", and at the same time whether he can trust others and stimulate each other's potential. Emotional intelligence includes self-control, enthusiasm, persistence, and the ability to be self-motivated and self-motivated.


How is it different to IQ??

The relationship between EQ and IQ is like a mountain. IQ is just the foot of the mountain and the foundation for future performance. Without IQ, many skills cannot be learned. EQ determines how high a person can climb, and EQ is proportional to the degree of excellence. And IQ is difficult to change, EQ can be improved through training.


Why is it important?

EQ determines how difficult it is to survive in society, and a high EQ can lead to a more successful life.

Identity Value

Something I have thinking about in terms of value and identity is curiosity. Most of my life experience is made up of curiosity. I am curious about many things and want to explore to find out what I really want. Just like many years ago, the problem that bothered me the most was that I didn’t know what I wanted. Later, it was curiosity that led to my life experience, and it was the experience that shaped who I am now. Gradually, I know what I want and gradually understand myself, from the excessive self-blame to reconciliation with myself. I believe curiosity can take a person anywhere, no matter physical or imagined.

I have been slowly shaping my life with good habits in recent years, little by little. Although everyone is not perfect, we can still make ourselves a little better than we were yesterday. Learning to code is also a part of shaping oneself. Learning coding can learn how to think and how to solve problems, which extends to all aspects of life, which is a change in thinking mode. Isn't that what EDA intends to teach us? I'm glad my friend recommended EDA to me. Very much looking forward to the day I officially become a developer!
